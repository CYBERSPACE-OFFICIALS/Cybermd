Everything your group needs

Group Management

Kick, add, promote, demote, mute, tag all members, revoke links and control every aspect of your group automatically.

🎞

Media Downloader

Download videos and audio from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook — no watermarks, instant delivery.

AI Powered

Chat with GPT, generate AI images, translate text, convert text to speech and fetch song lyrics on demand.

Sticker Tools

Convert any image or video to a sticker, turn stickers back to images, extract audio from video and add custom pack names.
